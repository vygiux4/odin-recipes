Greek Chicken and Potato Bowl

Mojo-marinated roast chicken, just like grandma used to

make!Serve with white rice, Serve with white rice with

Cuban bread or fresh rolls. You can use half the mojo

recipe for a smaller bird, drumsticks, or thighs.

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  1. 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  2. 3 tablespoons lime juice
  3. 3 tablespoons orange juice
  4. 1 (6 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
